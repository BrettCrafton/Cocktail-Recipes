Name: (Example) Whiskey Sour

❮ ❯

Ingredients

• Blended whiskey
• Lemon
• Powdered sugar
• Cherry
• Lemon

Instructions

Shake with ice. Strain into chilled glass, garnish and serve. If served 'On the rocks', strain ingredients into old-fashioned glass filled with ice.
